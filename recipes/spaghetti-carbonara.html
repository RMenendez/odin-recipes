Spaghetti carbonara

Home

Description

Spaghetti carborara. One of the most famous pasta dishes


Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino cheese
  • Spaghetti
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut guanciale in small cubes and put it in a pan until they are coocked. Reserve
  2. Grat the pecorino cheese
  3. Boil the spaghetti in abundant water with salt. Leave them "al dente"
  4. In a bowl mix the eggs (one per person), the grated pecorino, and the pepper
  5. Add some water from the spaghetti to obtain the desired consistency of the sauce
  6. When ready, pour the spaghetti and remove to incoporate the sauce
  7. Serve the spaghetti and add some guanciale and some more cheese on top
