Huevos fritos con patatas

Home

Description

Huevos fritos con patatas is a very simple recipe by it is one of my favourites and one of the classics master dishes in spanish cuisine


Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Potatos
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Put abundant olive oil in pan
  2. When heated, but not too much, put the potatos that you would have previously cleaned and cut in fries format
  3. Fry the potatos until they start to get brown in the edges, and then retire them to a plate and put some salt
  4. Now fry the eggs (one or two per person) also puting some salt
  5. Serve hot
